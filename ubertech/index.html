ABOUT UBERTECH

ACE Club's flagship event, "Ubertech," is a highly successful National Level Technical Symposium, running since 2011. With 11 editions so far, it attracts 1000+ participants nationwide. Ubertech offers diverse events across technical, non-technical, and gaming domains, along with industry trend workshops. Scheduled for September 26th and 27th of 2024, Ubertech promises two days of learning, networking, and fun, welcoming students from all colleges to showcase their talents and active participation.
